## RECORDING NOTES FOR PRESENTER

### Setup before recording
- Complete onboarding with: **Parent: Priya Sharma / Child: Arjun / DOB: March 2023** (25 months)
- Have a pre-filled questionnaire session ready for the post-assessment Dashboard section (so you can show both the empty and filled states)
- Have LM Studio running with `gemma-4-e4b` loaded before the Insights section
- For the camera screening section, have a second device or a printed face nearby to show face detection working
- On the Referrals page, pre-select **Delhi** as the state

### Screen flow checklist
1. Fresh onboarding → Language → Names → DOB
2. Home (pre-assessment state) — show both CTAs
3. Milestones questionnaire — show Q1 (critical item), section transition banner, Why It Matters panel, dot progress
4. Observe — intro, Scenario 1 instruction card, checklist items, Results with fused score
5. Insights — hero + risk card + fused score + AI loading spinner → AI cards + domain profile + expandable about section
6. Camera Screening — intro tasks → task in progress with countdown → results
7. Referrals — state picker → Delhi → referral cards → expand one card to show "What to say" + documents
8. Profile — child card + session timeline + language toggle (show Hindi) + PDF export (show PDF briefly)
9. Closing montage

### Timing notes
- Section 4 (Questionnaire) is the longest — move briskly after showing Q1 in detail; skip Q2–Q18 with a fast-forward visual
- The AI insight loading in Section 6 will take ~15–30s on Gemma 4 — cut around it or use a pre-loaded state
- The camera screening section does not need to complete a full 60s per task — show the UI and a 5-second clip per task, then cut to results
